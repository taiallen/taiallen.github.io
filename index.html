Hey You!!

It's me, Tairaya (she/her) the overseas foodie. This webpage a project to help me learn HTML and CSS. That's right, I'm a baby dev!

This project is a showcase of all the amazing food I ate (and photographed) during a trip to the UK and Europe back in 2019. Doesn't it look amazing?!

Anyway, enough about you, lets talk about ME. I'm the Studio Assistant at Conflare, a web development and brand design studio, the Social Media Coordinator at CD Forum, a non-profit organization for Black art and artists, an occcasional photographer, photo retoucher and videographer, and a self-proclaimed chef and foodie.

When I'm not doing any of those things, you can find me wrapped up in a fuzzy blanket learning French on Duolingo.
